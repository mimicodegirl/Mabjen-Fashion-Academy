Adeleke Yoshola Lydia is a graduate of Graduate and Counselling from Ekiti State University. After graduating from the university, she found her passion for fashion while she was undergoing NYSC. She decided to enroll in a roadside tailor shop to gain basic skills. However, the knowledge she got there was not enough and then decided to enroll in a fashion school at Abuja to further hone her skills. Yoshola is also a teacher and in the last few years, she has used her several birthed platforms to train & graduate over 50 youths that are now skilled in the fashion industry. Her hobbies singing, cooking and in her spare time she sings for the choir in her church.
